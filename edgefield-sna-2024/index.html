What is Degree Centrality?

  • Degree centrality tells us the number of immediate friendship ties each student has in the class.
  • It is best used for finding very connected nodes (i.e. popular students) who are likely to hold most information or can quickly connect with the rest of the network (i.e. class).

What is Betweeness Centrality?

  • Betweeness centrality considers the position where each node (i.e. student) is placed within the network.
  • Students with high betweeness centrality controls the flow of information, and can greatly influence the class by choosing to withhold or distort information he/she receives.
  • Captures the most important students in the network (i.e. the leaders or the most influential students).
